FIVE LARGEST U.S. PROTESTS IN HISTORY

Caption: People protesting the police killing of George Floyd.

The tradition of protests that demand progressive change in the United States continues.

Here are the country's top five protests in terms of attendace:

  1. George Floyd protests nationwide in 2020 with 26 million protestors.
  2. Women's march in 2017 drew about 5.6 million people nationwide.
  3. March For Our Lives, a student led protest in 2018 against gun violence, drew about 2 million people.
  4. Women's march in 2018 drew about 1.5 million people.
  5. March on Washington for Lesbian, Gay and Bi Equal Rights and Liberation drew about 1 million people in 1991.
